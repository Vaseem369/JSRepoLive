Day 9 — ES6+ Essentials

Template invoice line, destructuring scores and nested user, rest parameters — then spread merge and applyPrefs.

Task 1 Template literal sentence builder

With item, price, tax, build one sentence (with ₹) like: The Laptop costs ₹60000 + ₹10800 GST = ₹70800. Compute GST and total inside the template — do not pre-assign totals.

Bonus: a multiline template that puts each amount on its own line.
